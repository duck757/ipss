CCTV Video Surveillance

Cameras for video surveillance are a key component of modern security systems. Installing high-resolution cameras, both indoor and outdoor, enables continuous monitoring and recording of all events around protected areas. This technology not only facilitates passive observation but also allows for active intervention through features such as facial recognition, behavior analysis, and integration with alarm systems.

  • • High-resolution indoor & outdoor cameras
  • • 24/7 continuous monitoring & recording
  • • Facial recognition technology
  • • Behavior analysis & alarm integration
